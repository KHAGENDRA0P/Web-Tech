Gulab Jamun

Ingreadients

  • Sugar
  • Khoya
  • Maida
  • Milk

Process

  1. Take the grated Khoya. Add baking soda and maida. Combine them gently using a spoon.
  2. Add a teaspoon of milk to it. Make sure that the dough is a bit softer .
  3. Divide the dough into equal parts and make smooth surfaced balls out of it.
  4. Now heat ghee in a pan over medium flame and add the Jamun balls to deep-fry them until it becomes golden brown.
  5. Now drain and let them cool for few minutes. Then immerse them in the warm sugar syrup for at least 30 minutes.
  6. Hot and tasty Gulab Jamun is ready to serve.

Masala Paneer Roll

Ingreadients

  • rotis/ parathas
  • paneer
  • mayonnaise
  • chilli/aamchur powder

Process

  1. Finely chop or crumble the paneer and set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, combine all the ingredients of the stuffing. Add the paneer last.
  3. Divide the dough into equal parts and make smooth surfaced balls out of it.
  4. Roast the rotis/ parathas as usual and then spread around 1 tsp of mayonnaise on each.
  5. Add the masala paneer filling inside each, roll tightly and serve with ketchup and/or chutney.
